Technical Architecture

Inside the robot.

  • ESP32-S3 · 240 MHz Brain — dual-core, built-in WiFi, ~€5
  • MEMS Microphone Voice capture — noise-filtered, Whisper STT
  • HC-SR04 × 3 Ultrasonic — obstacle detection, 4m range
  • DC Motors × 2 + L298N Differential drive — autonomous navigation
  • SG90 Servo Head rotation — tracks guest before moving
  • LiPo 2500 mAh 4h active · 12h standby · auto-dock charging
